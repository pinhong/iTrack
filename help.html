Help

To Add a New Injury:

To add a new injury select "Add Injury" in the menu. Then select the athlete name and input the proper information about the newly recorded injury.


To See Your Schedule:

Click on "Schedule" in the drop down menu. In this page you will be able to see the open and booked times for treatment.


Find an Athlete's Profile:

To find a an ahtlete's profile you must click on "Athlete List" in the drop down menu. In this page you can find an athlete's profile by either by search or scrollin through the list. Once you have found the athlete who you are looking for, click on their name and their profile will be displayed.
